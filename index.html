Employees receiving notice of termination for any reason shall be allowed a reasonable amount of time (not less than 15 minutes) before the end of the regular work day to assemble their tools in addition to the normal pick-up time prevailing on the job.

First 40 Hours:Employer can reject or discharge for any reason

After 40 hours: Employer can disharge for just cause only

Section 30

Other than on the first day of dispatch, in which case 2 hours shall apply, workers who report for work, for whom no employment is provided, shall be entitled to 4 hours pay, except where bad weather conditions beyond the control of the Individual Employer prevents employment. Except as hereinafter provided, carpenters who start work, but are discharged between the hours of 8:00 A.M. and 12:00 noon, shall receive 4 hours pay; carpenters starting work at 8:00 A.M. who are discharged between the hours of 12:00 noon and 4:30 P.M. shall receive pay only for hours worked.

Carpenters discharged on the first day of employment for inefficiency, insubordination or intoxication, shall receive pay only for hours worked.

Carpenters who voluntarily quit shall receive pay only for hours worked.
